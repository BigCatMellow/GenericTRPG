Rules Quick Reference
Activation: 1 Move + 1 Action (either order). Or Run (9", → Spent, no Action).
Readiness: Ready → Committed → Spent. End-of-round: Spent→Committed, Committed→Ready.
Conditions: Guarded · Pinned · Exposed · Overwhelmed (Pinned+Exposed combined)
Attacks: Quick 1 dmg · Standard 2 dmg · Heavy 3 dmg (→ Spent)
Dodge (4+): declared before hit roll. Quick = 5+, Heavy = 3+. Pinned or Committed +1 harder.
Brace (4+): declared after hit. Reduces dmg 1 (Guarded: 2). Crit: +1 extra reduction.
Overwhelmed: Pinned + Exposed → next successful attack deals +1 dmg, then remove Exposed.
Objective: Control within 3". Pinned = 0. Securing = counts as 2. Score from Round 2. 1VP/round. First to 3VP wins.
Recover: Remove Pinned OR Remove Exposed OR improve readiness one step.
Hold: Become Guarded. Remove Exposed. Readiness unchanged.
